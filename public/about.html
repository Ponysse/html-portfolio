About Me

Hello!

My name is Denisse Barrera, I am 33 years old.

In the last year I discovered that I am capable of doing what I set my mind to if I am really interested and motivated. In that discovery emerged fears and doubts about my own self, but I came to the conclusion that I really have nothing to lose by trying and finding challenges along the way.

One of the things that motivate me the most are the challenges, but at the same time they scare me a lot because I fear not being able to overcome them. It was quite hard accepting that because I thought it was a bit pathetic that, after all, everything we read and hear about self-improvement is true, there is no greater enemy than oneself.

After many months of convincing myself that this is true, I am here, trying and at the same time slowly learning and advancing a little bit every day. It's better than staying where I was, isn't it?

Anyway, this process excites me and I hope it stays that way.
